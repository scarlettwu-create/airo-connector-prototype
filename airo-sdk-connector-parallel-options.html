Parallel entry treatments

Six ways to show the two choices as equal weight. Same parallel intent as today, different visual containers. Each is a live mockup at roughly real width.

All of these keep AIRO and manual equal. They differ in how much they explain each path, how much vertical space they take, and how "clickable" each side feels. If you want to nudge toward AIRO later, treatment 3 (tinted halves) is the easiest to bias without a full redesign.
